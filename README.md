# ASU Reclamation Project

**LIVE SITE:** [https://sites.google.com/view/asureclamationproject/home?authuser=2](https://sites.google.com/view/asureclamationproject/home?authuser=2)

**Tagline:** Reclaiming urban spaces to reconnect people, nature, and place  
A community-centered project transforming underused urban environments into vibrant living gardens through art, storytelling, and digital innovation.

---

## About

The **ASU Reclamation Project** is developed by the California Center for Climate Change Education in collaboration with **Arizona State University**.  
This initiative focuses on **urban revitalization** by integrating:

- Community narratives  
- Environmental design  
- Digital technologies such as 3D modeling and XR (extended reality)

---

## Project Overview

**Purpose:** Reimagine and activate underused urban spaces to foster stronger connections between people, nature, and place.  

**Approach:** Combines **community engagement**, **storytelling**, **digital tools**, and **climate-focused design**.

**Collaborators:**

- California Center for Climate Change Education  
- Arizona State University  
- Architecture, Engineering and Construction Fellows 2025

---

## Key Features

- **Site Analysis & Context:** In-depth examination of urban locations  
- **Digital Twin & XR:** Use of digital twins and XR to visualize and simulate design scenarios  
- **Community & Storytelling:** Emphasis on local narratives to guide design and engagement
